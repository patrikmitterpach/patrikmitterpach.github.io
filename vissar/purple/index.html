Violet family — primary
All brand interactions, logo mark, navigation, and UI chrome. Violet (#8B6FFF) is the identity anchor. Use Violet Lt on dark surfaces for text, Violet Dk for pressed states and borders.
Ember family — accent
Ember (#FF7A35) drives call-to-action, power states, and heat indicators — it's the brand's energy. Flame Lt only on dark backgrounds for legible text. Ember Dk for borders and hover.

05 — Voice & Tone
Precise
Every word earns its place. Specs are stated exactly. No fluff. Technical claims are always verifiable and cited.
Charged
Copy carries momentum. Declarative, direct, impossible to ignore. We don't ask — we state. Energy without noise.
Forward
Vissar lives at the frontier. Language anticipates what's next. The voice never looks backward or hedges needlessly.
Don't write
"Our innovative platform helps teams maybe improve their operational workflows potentially."
Write this instead
"Sub-centimeter precision. 6,200m certified ceiling. Deploy in under four minutes."
